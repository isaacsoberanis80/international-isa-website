# International ISA — Service Packages & Pricing

One page. When a prospect asks "what does it cost?", the answer comes from
here — no improvising on calls. Every package sells an outcome (answered
phones, followed-up leads, hours back), never the technology.

---

## Package 1 — Office Support Agent (the core offer)

**Who it's for:** Any business hiring (or wishing they could hire) a
secretary, receptionist, office admin, dispatcher, or inside sales agent.

**What they get:** A trained remote professional (English-first, bilingual
EN/ES included at no extra charge) handling calls, scheduling, follow-ups,
lead qualification, quotes/POs, CRM updates, and customer service.

**Price:** **$18/hour**, minimum 20 hrs/week.
- 20 hrs/wk ≈ $1,560/mo — vs ~$3,700+/mo fully-loaded cost of a $45K hire
- The pitch line: *"Skip the hire. Same work, half the cost, live this week."*

**Term:** Month-to-month. No long contracts — makes the first yes easy.

---

## Package 2 — AI Agent + Human Rep Bundle (the premium offer)

**Who it's for:** Businesses with real lead volume that lose deals to slow
follow-up (high-volume real estate teams, 24hr trades companies).

**What they get:** An AI agent that answers/qualifies instantly, 24/7 +
a human rep who takes over every warm conversation. AI speed, human close.

**Price:** **$5,000 setup + $18/hr for the human hours.**
- Setup covers building the AI agent on their tools, scripts, integration
- The pitch line: *"The AI answers in 5 seconds at 2am. The human closes."*

---

## Package 3 — Revenue Share (the no-risk door-opener)

**Who it's for:** Skeptical prospects who won't pay upfront; strong teams
where we're confident in volume.

**What they get:** Same as Package 1 or 2, but paid as **20–50% of new
revenue we generate** (negotiated per deal, based on their close rate and
deal size). Use sparingly — only where deal flow is verifiable.

---

## Package 4 — Automation & CRM Setup (the expansion offer)

**Sell only after packages 1–3 land, or as an add-on to an existing
client.** Not a door-opener — businesses buy people and outcomes before
they buy systems.

**What they get (pick per client):**
- CRM setup or cleanup (HubSpot/Zoho/GoHighLevel/Airtable — recommend
  no-code first, custom only when nothing fits)
- Lead routing + follow-up automation
- Email/appointment automation
- Reporting dashboard

**Price:** **$1,500–$5,000 one-time per project** (scoped), or bundled
free-ish into Package 2 setup to sweeten a close.

**Proof-of-work for the pitch:** our own stack — internationalisa.com, the
lead-scoring CRM dashboard, Morgan (AI advisor with live web research that
writes to the CRM), automated daily lead-gen and SMS reporting. Built
in-house; we sell what we already run.

---

## Rules
- Always quote from this sheet. If a deal needs custom pricing, decide
  *after* the call, not on it.
- Never discount Package 1 below $15/hr — below that the model breaks.
- Every proposal states the outcome in dollars ("replaces a $45K hire",
  "recovers the ~62% of calls that go unanswered"), never a feature list.
