# Discovery Call Script — International ISA (15 min)

Goal: understand their pipeline pain, qualify, and book the close — not
pitch features. You talk 30%, they talk 70%.

## 1. Opener (30 sec)
"Thanks for taking the time. I'll keep this to 15 minutes. My goal today
is just to understand how leads flow through your business — and I'll tell
you honestly at the end whether we can help. Fair?"

## 2. Discovery questions (8 min — let them talk)
1. "Walk me through what happens when a new lead comes in. Who touches it first, and how fast?"
2. "How many leads a month, roughly? From where — Zillow, sign calls, sphere, referrals?"
3. "What percentage would you guess never gets a follow-up past the first attempt?"
4. "Who does your admin today — listings, paperwork, CRM? What does that cost you in hours?"
5. "Have you tried hiring an ISA or assistant before? What happened?"
6. (Money) "What's your average commission per closing?"

## 3. The math moment (2 min)
Use THEIR numbers: "So if you're getting ~[X] leads and even [Y]% more got
worked properly, at your average commission of $[Z], that's $___ a year
sitting in your CRM. Our service costs about $1,560/month. That's the
whole pitch."

## 4. Present ONE package (2 min)
- Pain = follow-up → Package 1: dedicated ISA, $18/hr, 20 hrs/wk min, month-to-month.
- Pain = volume + speed → Package 2: AI + human bundle, $5K setup + $18/hr.
Quote from SERVICE_PACKAGES.md. Never improvise pricing.

## 5. Objections (quick answers)
- **"You're new / no reviews"** → "True, and that's why you get founder-level service and month-to-month terms. If we don't earn it, you walk. You're taking a small risk for a first-client level of attention."
- **"How do I know the quality?"** → "First week is onboarding on YOUR scripts and standards. You hear the calls. If it's not right, we fix it or you cancel."
- **"Too expensive"** → "Compared to what — the $45K hire, or the leads dying in your CRM? At [their commission], one saved deal pays for months."
- **"I need to think about it"** → "Of course. What specifically would you need to see to feel good about a yes?" (Isolate the real objection.)
- **"Offshore VAs are $8/hr"** → "And they're worth it for data entry. Your leads call back a real conversation — sales-trained, North America hours, English-first. That gap is where commissions live."

## 6. Close (1 min)
"Based on what you told me, here's what I'd do: [1 sentence plan].
Want to start with 20 hours a week and see what a month does?"
- Yes → send proposal same day (PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md), start date within 7 days.
- Maybe → book a specific follow-up date before hanging up. Never "I'll call you."
