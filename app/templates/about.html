About International ISA

We help real estate agents and brokers save time and close more deals by handling the sales work that eats their calendar.

International ISA was built on a simple idea: realtors are better at closing than at cold calling. We supply trained, bilingual Inside Sales Agents who handle prospecting, lead qualification, and follow-up, so agents can spend their time where it matters most — in front of clients.

Every agent we place is trained on needs-based selling, not scripts read off a page. We focus on real conversations, real follow-through, and real results — treating every lead the way we'd want to be treated.
