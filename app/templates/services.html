Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from hiring my own ISA?

You get the sales capacity without the recruiting, training, payroll, or turnover risk. We handle sourcing and coaching; you get a ready-to-work real estate ISA embedded in your team for $18/hour instead of a $45K+ hire.

What if the agent isn't the right fit?

We work with you during onboarding to make sure the agent understands your market and standards, and we stay involved with ongoing coaching rather than handing off and disappearing.

Do I need to provide leads, or can you source them?

Either. Your ISA can work the leads you already buy (Zillow, realtor.com, Ylopo, PPC), prospect new ones, or both — we build the process around how your team already sells.

Is this only for English-speaking clients?

Our ISAs work English-first, and bilingual (English/Spanish) support is included at no extra charge when your market needs it.

How do I get started?

Book a free demo. We'll talk through your market and pipeline, and if it's a fit, we start matching you with an agent.
